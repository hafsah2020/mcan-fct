About Us

MCAN FCT is dedicated to supporting Muslim corpers in the Federal Capital Territory through community building, capacity development, and spiritual growth. We offer a range of programs and activities designed to help members thrive during their service year and beyond.

Our Pillars

We focus on three key areas to support our members:

auto_stories

Spiritual Growth

Offering spiritual guidance, religious discussions, and activities to foster a deeper understanding and connection with faith.

Community Building

Creating a supportive network for Muslim corpers to connect, share experiences, and build lasting relationships.

Capacity Development

Providing skill-building workshops, seminars, and mentorship programs to enhance professional and personal development.
